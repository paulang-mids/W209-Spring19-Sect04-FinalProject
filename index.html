Concept

Context

  • Environmental Protection Agency has 20 year project to assess risk associated with pollutants that are emitted across the U.S.
  • Also assess long term effects of pollutants on individualsâ€™ health
  • Prior to 2000, data was only available to regulators and policy makers
  • Since 2000, data is available to public but is not easily accessible and consumable - multiple Access tables
  • Lindsey et al developed prototype solution combining data for one year and making accessible via API*

Objective

  • Build upon work of Lindsey et al by creating interactive visualization allowing the audience to easily explore and gain actionable insights from the pollutant/risk data
  • Allow for multiple filtering options to provide flexibility
